ABOUT.

Hey! My name is Liam - also known as limegorilla, Lamp or Truck. The last two get somewhat weird. I'm a photo/videographer, coder and Software Development apprentice from the UK. I make things - like everything you see on this site! It's all been custom made (with help from StackOverflow :) ) for this site, including animations, illustrations and the photographs - some of which were shot by my friends - see the acknowlegements page.
