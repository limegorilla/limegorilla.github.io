"What's a LimeGorilla?"

People ask me this a lot. My username is rather... unique, to say the least. When I was chosing it, I had no idea that so many damn people would ask about it.

The long and short of it is, there is no hidden meaning. No hidden gorilla that I worship. It was just my favorite colour and a random word from a generator.
It is a memorable name though - so I haven't done to badly.
